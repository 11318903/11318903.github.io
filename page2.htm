WHY MY HOBBY IS READING?

So, the question is, why is my hobby reading?
This reading hobby is present in me when I see my mother and sister reading novels and they are obsessed with ignoring others. I was intrigued by what they did, so I tried to take a novel for an internal study of the novel and it turned out that the novel was not as fun as a picture book because it contained 100% writing. And at that time I was still in primary school in grade 4.

When I was in 6th grade, I tried to take it to read and my mother did not allow little children like me to read such a novel because the content of the novel was not clean for the boys to look at. so, I persuaded my mother to read a little and gave a reason to look and sooner or later I would get bored. Due to the reason, my mother allowed me to read but in her monitoring, and it turned out that I managed to finish the novel in 3 days. In fact, day after day I made reading that my hobby and I managed to finish 1 thin or thick novel in 1 day and now I can afford to read 2 to 3 books in a day.

What I got after starting to read the book is, it helps in my writing where I got an A in writing essays in UPSR, PT3 and even SPM. My heart also calmed down after reading the book and my imagination also expanded.
